Robust Training Strategy

Apollo achieved exceptional performance through a comprehensive robust training approach designed to prevent the NaN crashes that plagued earlier versions:

Training Configuration

  • Epochs: 100 (completed successfully)
  • Batch Size: 128 samples
  • Learning Rate: 0.0005 (base) to 0.002 (max)
  • Dataset: 100K train, 20K validation, 10K test samples
  • Scheduler: CosineAnnealingLR with warm restarts

Stability Features

  • Stable Focal Loss: Custom implementation with epsilon=1e-7 for numerical stability
  • NaN Detection: Comprehensive checking at every forward pass
  • Gradient Clipping: 0.5 threshold to prevent exploding gradients
  • Conservative Weight Init: Xavier uniform with gain=0.5
  • Layer Normalization: Feature normalization for stable training
  • HuberLoss: More robust than MSE for regression tasks

Training Progression

Early Training (Epochs 1-20): Rapid improvement from 82.2% to 99.3%

Mid Training (Epochs 21-60): Steady refinement reaching 99.7%+

Final Polish (Epochs 61-100): Consistent 99.9%+ with excellent stability

Final Result: 99.92% test accuracy with zero NaN crashes
